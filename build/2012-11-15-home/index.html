New Ruins has found a home Nov 15, 2012

Above we are celebrating our access to the rights to use the land behind us on Venado beach. Elena, Fernando, Angela, Nic and Majo champagne in hand and history in the making.

Its been a long and arduous process to get here. But here starts the beginning to a new era for all of us here at New Ruins. It could have maybe been faster or more decisive, but we are in mexico so we didn't do it that way. A lot of time spent looking at many small beaches and the communities around them. Flip-flopping, back pedaling, talking ourselves or oneself in a circles; we've tried it all. But alas we have arrived, Home. For those of us who were bored sitting there with their engines running, now find themselves in front of a giant slab of stone waiting to be sculpted.
